ABOUT ANIME

Masashi Kishimoto's shonen manga tells the story of a rowdy and restless teenage ninja, Naruto Uzumaki, who dreams of achieving universal recognition and becoming Hokage - the head of his village and the strongest ninja. In order to gain the respect of those around him, he has to go through thousands of obstacles: ninja exams, various missions and battles.
